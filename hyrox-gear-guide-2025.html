HYROX GEAR GUIDE 2025

What gear do real HYROX athletes rely on? We asked 45 members from our community.

Choosing the right gear for HYROX can make a real difference on race day. We surveyed 45 members from the Resilience Fitness community — athletes who've competed at HYROX Toronto, Houston, and beyond — to find out what they actually wear and use. Here's what came back.

TRAINING & RACE SHOES

The most important piece of gear for HYROX. You need a shoe that can handle running AND functional stations. Most of our athletes opt for a hybrid trainer or a dedicated HYROX-specific shoe.

  • Look for a wide toe box for stability
  • Carbon plate optional for race day
  • Break them in before race day
  • [SPECIFIC SHOE RECOMMENDATIONS PLACEHOLDER — add your picks]

LIFTING BELT

A belt can make a big difference on the sled push/pull and farmers carry stations. Many of our athletes wear one for race day even if they don't train with one.

  • 4-inch lever belt recommended
  • Only for the heavy stations
  • [BRAND RECOMMENDATIONS PLACEHOLDER]

GRIPS & GLOVES

Wall balls and sandbag carries can tear up your hands, especially late in the race. Gymnastics grips or lightweight gloves are popular in our community.

  • Gymnastics grips for wall ball
  • Lightweight gloves for sandbag
  • Consider sweat absorption
  • [RECOMMENDATIONS PLACEHOLDER]

TRAINING APPAREL

Comfort and range of motion over everything. You're running 8km and doing 8 functional workouts — you need gear that moves with you.

  • Compression shorts or tights for support
  • Breathable top — avoid cotton
  • Resilience gear available in-store and online
  • [PHOTO PLACEHOLDER — Resilience apparel]

RACE DAY NUTRITION

Don't overlook race day fuelling. HYROX takes 60-90 minutes — you'll want to come in fuelled and stay on top of hydration.

  • Eat 2-3 hours before race
  • Simple carbs for race morning
  • Electrolyte mix in your water bottle
  • Gels optional for longer finishers

"The right gear won't make you fast — but the wrong gear can slow you down. Prepare like a professional."

— Resilience Fitness Coaches
