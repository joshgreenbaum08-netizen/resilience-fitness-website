Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2023–11–05

Welcome to Resilience Fitness. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our members and visitors. Our Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how it is used, and the measures we take to ensure your personal information is treated appropriately.

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect information that you provide to us when you:

  • Sign up for a membership
  • Register for a class or event
  • Subscribe to our newsletters
  • Contact us with inquiries or customer support requests

The personal information we collect may include your name, email address, phone number, payment information, and health-related information as it pertains to your fitness goals.

2. Use of Personal Information

The personal information we collect is used to:

  • Process your membership and class registrations
  • Provide you with tailored fitness programs
  • Communicate with you about your account or services we offer
  • Send you promotional materials, with your consent
  • Improve our services and offerings

3. Disclosure of Personal Information

We will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law or as needed to provide you with services (e.g., payment processors, class scheduling software).

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Access and Correction

You have the right to access and correct your personal information. To request access or make corrections, please contact us at contact@resiliencefitness.ca.

6. Retention of Personal Information

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@resiliencefitness.ca
  • Phone: (437) 826-9080
  • Address: 1400 O'Connor Drive, East York, ON
